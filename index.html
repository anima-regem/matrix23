About Hackathon!

  • A hackathon is an event where people come together to create something new and innovative in a short amount of time.
  • Anyone can participate in a hackathon, regardless of their skill level or background.
  • Hackathons often have a specific theme or focus, such as technology, social good, or sustainability.
  • Participants usually work in teams, collaborating with others to develop their ideas and bring them to life.
  • Hackathons are a great way to learn new skills and gain experience in a particular field.
  • They can be a fun and social experience, with opportunities to meet new people and make connections with others who share your interests..
  • Hackathons often offer prizes or awards for the best projects, which can be a great motivator to do your best.
  • Even if you don't win, participating in a hackathon can be a valuable learning experience and a chance to build your portfolio or resume.
  • Above all, hackathons are about creativity, innovation, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible. So if you're curious, passionate, and willing to take on a challenge, then a hackathon might be just the thing for you!

Guidelines

  • The maximum number of participants in each team cannot exceed 4.
  • The teams registered must report for the offline hackathon at Government Engineering College Palakkad before 10:00 am on 22/03/2023.
  • The theme for the hackathon will be provided on the spot.
  • Hackathon will start at 10:30 am on 22/03/2023 and last until 10:30 am the next day, 23/03/2023, after which a presentation and evaluation will be conducted.
  • All work on a project should be done during the hackathon.
  • Teams can be disqualified from the competition at the organizer's discretion. Reasons might include but are not limited to breaking the Competition Rules or unsporting behaviour.

Keep in mind!

  • Each participant must first register individually.
  • Team formation will happen during the registration.
  • Food and Refreshments will be provided starting from a lunch on 22nd till a lunch on 23rd March.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops and other devices necessary.
  • The detailed schedule of the program will be released later.
  • Have fun tinkering.
